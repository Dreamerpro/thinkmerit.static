FAQ

1. How do I join ThinkMerit.in?

Ans: To be a part of ThinkMerit.in, one has to go through a simple registration procedure, that is absolutely free. The following steps are to be followed for registration.
Log-on to www.thinkmerit.in
Click on "Login" if you are an existing user
If you are a new user fill in your required details in the "Sign Up" box at the right panel of the home page.
Once completed, click the "Sign Up" button

Congrats! You are now a registered member of ThinkMerit.in

2. Why should I join ThinkMerit.in?

Ans: ThinkMerit.in is an online education portal which is dedicated to provide quality learning and assessment solutions to students of class 11 & 12th of science background who are also preparing for engineering competitive examination. ThinkMerit.in caters to the learning needs of students through its high-quality study content in the form of multimedia-enriched study material, question bank with detailed solution and concept, videos lectures, test series, ask an expert and career counselling.
On signing up with ThinkMerit.in you get full access to our study materials.

3. Is studying on ThinkMerit.in sufficient?

Ans: Yes, absolutely studying on ThinkMerit.in is sufficient to build solid concept for every topic and for performing good in all the engineering competitive examination.

4. For studying on ThinkMerit.in, do we need any subscription.?

Ans: ThinkMerit.in is completely free for all the students. Just become a registered member of ThinkMerit.in by signing up and enter the world of knowledge.
